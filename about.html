Our Story

Bal Deeksha Sadan School is located in Gwarko, Lalitpur. It offers Ten Plus Two in Science, Management, and Humanities streams with affiliation from the National Examination Board.

Bal Deeksha Sadan (BDS), the school with 37 years of glorious history, focuses on the holistic development of children required to become globally competitive and humanistic citizens.

The school has good physical infrastructures as well as ample playgrounds required for sports and other activities for the students.

The school believes that children today are responsible citizens of tomorrow. However, the nourishment of these buds cannot blossom unless they receive the proper academic environment that will enable them to become acquainted with the nation, their society, and their family.

It has a qualified team of instructors who are continuously supervised and guided by a group of academicians, social workers, and professionals involved in different disciplines of society. The school employs innovative practices in teaching and learning, helping students prove themselves outstanding in every corner of the world.
